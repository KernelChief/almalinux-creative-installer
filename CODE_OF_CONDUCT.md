This project follows the **Contributor Covenant (v2.1)**.

Our goal is to keep the AlmaLinux Creative Installer community welcoming,
respectful, and safe for everyone.

## üåç Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## ‚úÖ Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward others
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Taking responsibility, apologizing, and learning from mistakes
- Focusing on what is best for the overall community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances of any kind
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Other conduct reasonably considered inappropriate in a professional setting

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards

issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces and also when an

## ‚öñÔ∏è Enforcement Guidelines

Community leaders will follow these Community Impact Guidelines:

### 1) Correction
**Community Impact:** Inappropriate language or other unprofessional/unwelcome behavior.

**Consequence:** A private written warning with clarity on why the behavior was inappropriate.

### 2) Warning
**Community Impact:** A violation through a single incident or series of actions.

**Consequence:** A warning with consequences for continued behavior and temporary limits on interaction.

### 3) Temporary Ban
**Community Impact:** A serious violation of community standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior.

**Consequence:** A temporary ban from interaction or public communication with the community.

### 4) Permanent Ban
**Community Impact:** A pattern of violations, sustained inappropriate behavior, harassment, or aggression/disparagement toward classes of individuals.

**Consequence:** A permanent ban from public interaction within the community.
